## Features

- Customer Order Tracking: Customers can easily track the status of their orders in real time.
- Add Notes to Orders: Both customers and employees can add notes or comments to specific orders for better communication.
- Employee Dashboard: Employees can view and manage all customer orders efficiently.
- Order Requests: Employees can create and manage new service or product requests directly from the dashboard.
- Detailed Reports: Generate comprehensive reports for orders, services, and performance metrics.
- Service Showcase: Display all company services (UPVC Windows, Glass Facades, WPC Doors, etc.) with detailed descriptions and images.
- Secure Login System: Role-based access for customers, employees, and administrators.

---

## Technologies Used

- HTML5: For structuring all web pages with modern semantic elements.
- CSS3: For creating a responsive and visually appealing design.
- JavaScript (JS): For adding interactivity, dynamic content, and real-time updates.
- Firebase: Used for backend services such as authentication, database, hosting, and real-time order tracking.
