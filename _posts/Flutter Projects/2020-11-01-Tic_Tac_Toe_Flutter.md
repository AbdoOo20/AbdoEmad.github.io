# ❌⭕ Tic Tac Toe

An interactive Tic Tac Toe game built with Flutter. You can play against the computer (AI) or with a friend (two players).

## Features
- Play against the computer (simple AI)
- Two players mode on the same device
- Simple and attractive user interface
- Easily restart the game

## Play Modes
- **Player vs Computer:**
  - When the "Two Player" switch is OFF, you play against the computer (O).
- **Two Players:**
  - When the "Two Player" switch is ON, two players can take turns on the same device.
